Privacy Policy — Blockade Runner
Last updated: April 18, 2026

Blockade Runner ("the app", "we", "us") is a single-player arcade game. This policy explains, in plain language, what data the app does and does not handle.

Short version
The app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. Everything you see on the Captain's Log — earnings, runs completed, upgrades, achievements, daily-challenge streak — is stored locally on your device only.

What we store on your device
The following information is saved in the app's local storage on your phone or tablet:

Total in-game earnings and last-run earnings
Number of runs completed per map and difficulty
Best Survival-mode time per map
Daily-challenge streak and last result
Upgrade levels (hull, chaff)
Selected and unlocked ship skins
Unlocked achievements
This data never leaves your device. It is removed when you uninstall the app or when you tap "Scuttle Log" inside the Captain's Log screen.

What we do not collect
We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other identifier.
We do not use analytics, tracking pixels, advertising IDs, or third-party SDKs that transmit data.
We do not show advertisements.
We do not offer in-app purchases.
We do not require an account to play.
Optional platform leaderboards
If you choose to opt in to leaderboards, your high scores may be submitted to:

Apple Game Center (iOS), governed by Apple's privacy policy at https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/
Google Play Games Services (Android), governed by Google's privacy policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy
Score submission is optional. If you do not sign in to Game Center or Play Games, no scores are sent off the device.

Children's privacy
The app is rated for ages 10 and up. Because the app does not collect personal data from anyone, it is also compliant with COPPA and similar children's-privacy regulations.

Changes to this policy
If we ever change this policy, the updated version will be posted at the same URL with a new "Last updated" date.
